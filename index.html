Welcome to Mini Chess

Instructions:

- To play, click on a piece to select it and then click on a highlighted space to either move there or take an enemy piece.

- Press escape or backspace to cancel a move.

- You can only move white/black pieces when it is white's/black's turn.

- For the sake of simplicity, no special movements are allowed. (so no castling, pawns cannot move 2 spaces the first time they are moved, etc)

- Furthermore, to end the game the enemy king must be captured rather than checkmated.
